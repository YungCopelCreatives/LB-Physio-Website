Project Overview

  • Category: Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation
  • Date: November 5, 2024
  • Client: Ntando Mazibuko
  • Age: Not specified

Project Details

I treated a client with severe scapular muscle knots causing referred pain to the shoulder, collarbone, and neck, restricting neck mobility. Significant improvements in pain relief and mobility were noted, with self-management strategies for long-term recovery. The client appreciated the holistic and informative approach, recommending my services to others.

Knee Replacement Rehabilitation

Project Overview

  • Category: Post-Surgical Rehabilitation
  • Date: August 15, 2024
  • Client: Thabiso Khumalo
  • Age: 38

Project Details

I worked with a 38-year-old client following a knee replacement surgery. The treatment plan included pain management, swelling reduction, and progressive strengthening exercises. We gradually introduced functional mobility exercises to improve knee function and range of motion. Within 8 weeks, the client regained full knee mobility and was able to return to daily activities with improved strength and confidence. The client expressed great satisfaction with the rehabilitation process and recommended my services to others.
