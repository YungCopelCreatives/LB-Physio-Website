Our Physiotherapy Services

At Luyanda Buthelezi Physiotherapy, we offer a range of services tailored to meet your individual needs.

Craniotomy & Spinal Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Customized programs to restore strength, mobility, and functional independence after neurosurgical or spinal procedures.

Hip, Knee & Shoulder Replacements

Evidence-based post-surgical rehabilitation to optimize recovery and prevent complications.

Headache Management

Targeted techniques to alleviate tension headaches and chronic migraine symptoms.

Chronic Back & Neck Pain

Multimodal strategies combining manual therapy, exercise, and education for long-term pain relief.

Sports Injuries & Rehabilitation

Athlete-focused recovery plans to restore peak performance and prevent re-injury.
