Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered about physiotherapy and injuries.

1. What conditions do you treat at Luyanda Buthelezi Physiotherapy?

We specialize in post-operative rehab (spinal, hip/knee replacements), sports injuries, chronic back/neck pain, pediatric care, and headaches. Our holistic approach ensures tailored treatment for all ages.

2. Why should I choose your clinic?

We combine clinical expertise with compassion, offering specialized pediatric care, BokSmart accreditation, and a commitment to restoring functional independence. Our core values—integrity, transparency, and patience—guide every session.

3. Do I need a doctor's referral to book an appointment?

No referral is needed! You can book directly with us for assessment and treatment.

4. How do I book an appointment?

Call us at (+27) 073 948 0811, email luyandabschemes@gmail.com, or visit our website to request a booking.

5. What should I bring to my first session?

Bring your ID, medical aid card (if applicable), and any relevant medical reports (e.g., surgery notes, X-rays).

6. How long is a typical physiotherapy session?

Sessions last 45–60 minutes, depending on your treatment plan.

7. Do you offer emergency or after-hours care?

Yes! Contact us directly for urgent cases, and we'll prioritize your needs.

8. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, medical aid, and immediate wire transfers (subject to advance clearance and confirmation).

9. Are your facilities wheelchair-accessible?

Yes, our clinic in Northriding, Randburg, is fully accessible.

10. What makes your pediatric physiotherapy unique?

Our founder, Luyanda Buthelezi, is fully trained in pediatric care, using innovative techniques to improve children's mobility and functional independence.

11. Can you help with sports injury prevention?

Absolutely! We offer BokSmart-accredited strapping/taping courses and personalized rehab plans to keep athletes injury-free.

12. Do you treat chronic headaches?

Yes! We use manual therapy, posture correction, and exercises to reduce headache frequency and intensity.

13. What's involved in post-operative rehabilitation?

We design step-by-step recovery plans to rebuild strength, mobility, and confidence after surgeries like hip/knee replacements or craniotomies.

14. How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions required varies based on the individual and the nature of the injury. Your physiotherapist will create a personalized treatment plan and discuss the expected duration of therapy.

15. What should I wear to my physiotherapy appointment?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows for easy movement. Depending on your treatment, you may need to change into shorts or a tank top.

16. Do you offer home visits?

Yes, for patients with mobility challenges. Contact us to arrange a visit.

17. Can I join your strapping/taping workshops?

Yes! Our courses are open to athletes, coaches, and the public. Follow our social media for updates.

18. How do I leave a testimonial?

Email us or share your experience on Google Reviews. We value your feedback!

19. What are the conditions for wire transfers?

Wire transfers must be confirmed before your appointment via email. Funds must reflect in our account on the day of your session.

20. Do you offer payment plans for cash patients?

Yes! Discuss flexible payment options with our team based on your needs.
