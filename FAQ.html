We're Here to Answer Your Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about our physiotherapy services

Do I need a medical referral to see a physiotherapist?

While a referral from a doctor is not always necessary, many medical aid schemes require one to cover costs. If you have medical aid, we recommend checking with your provider or bringing a letter from your doctor. If paying privately, no referral is needed.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring: valid ID, medical referral (if you have one), medical aid card and details, any recent imaging (X-rays, scans), and a list of any medications you take. Wearing comfortable, loose clothing is recommended.

How long is each physiotherapy session?

Standard sessions are 30-45 minutes, depending on your condition and treatment plan. Your first assessment may take longer to ensure we gather all necessary information and develop a personalized recovery plan.

Will medical aid cover physiotherapy?

Most medical aid schemes do cover physiotherapy, but coverage varies. We can provide receipts for you to claim directly, or we can check your benefits beforehand. Please bring your medical aid details to your appointment.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions depends on your condition, severity, and recovery goals. After your initial assessment, we'll discuss a recommended treatment plan tailored to your needs. Some conditions improve in a few weeks, while others may require ongoing support.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for 24 hours' notice for cancellations. If you need to reschedule, please contact us as soon as possible. Last-minute cancellations may incur a cancellation fee. We understand that emergencies happen, so please communicate with us.

Do you treat children?

Yes, we specialize in pediatric physiotherapy. Children of all ages can benefit from our services, whether for developmental concerns, injuries, or post-surgical rehabilitation. We ensure a comfortable, child-friendly environment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, card payments (Visa, Mastercard), and bank transfers. We can also process medical aid claims directly in many cases. Ask about our payment options at the time of booking.

Can I book an appointment online or via WhatsApp?

Yes! You can book through our website appointment form or message us on WhatsApp at +27 73 948 0811. We'll respond quickly to confirm your session and answer any questions.

Do you offer after-hours appointments?

Yes, we do! We're open Monday to Friday from 14:00–18:00 and Saturday from 08:00–14:00. This allows working professionals and students to access care outside normal business hours. Sunday is closed.
